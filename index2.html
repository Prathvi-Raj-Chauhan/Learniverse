Fun Facts About Science

  1. Rainbows are made of seven colors: red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, and violet. Remember them as "ROYGBIV"!
  2. The Sun is a star that gives us heat and light. It's the closest star to Earth!
  3. Your heart beats faster when you exercise to pump more blood to your muscles.
  4. Chameleons and octopuses can change their skin color to blend with surroundings.
  5. There are more stars in the sky than grains of sand on Earth!
  6. A goldfish can remember things for up to 5 months—much longer than the myth suggests!
  7. Caterpillars transform into butterflies inside a cocoon. It’s called metamorphosis!
