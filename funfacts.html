Cool Science Facts

  • Did you know? The Earth is not a perfect sphere! It’s slightly flattened at the poles.
  • Fun Fact: A day on Venus is longer than a year on Venus!
  • Mind-blowing: Honey never spoils! Archaeologists have found pots of honey in ancient tombs that are still good to eat!
  • Amazing: Your body has around 60,000 miles of blood vessels! That's enough to circle the Earth twice!
  • Did you know? Some species of jellyfish are immortal. They can revert back to their early life stage and start over!
