The Solar System

Now let's us learn more about these planets.

Mercury
Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun.
Venus
Venus is similar in size to Earth but is much hotter.
Earth
Earth is the only planet known to support life.
Mars
Mars is known as the Red Planet due to its reddish color.
Jupiter
Jupiter is the largest planet in the Solar System.
Saturn
Saturn is famous for its beautiful rings.
Uranus
Uranus spins on its side and has a very cold atmosphere.
Neptune
Neptune is the farthest planet from the Sun and has strong winds.
