Parts of a Plant

Plants have various parts such as roots, stems, leaves, flowers, and fruits. Each part has a specific function that helps the plant grow and survive.


Types of Plants

There are different types of plants including trees, shrubs, herbs, climbers, and creepers. Each type has unique characteristics and grows in different environments.

Photosynthesis

Photosynthesis is the process by which plants make their food using sunlight, carbon dioxide, and water. This process occurs in the leaves of the plant.
